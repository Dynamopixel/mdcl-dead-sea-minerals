WHO WE ARE

Dead Sea: the ideal origin
of minerals

Israel's Dead Sea is the saltiest natural lake in the world. This characteristic makes it a unique source of dissolved mineral mixture. -Its waters have a high concentration of rich minerals, mainly calcium, magnesium, potassium and bromine. The extraction of these minerals occurs through a natural process through solar evaporation. The extracted minerals contain a particularly low content of heavy metals and are free of animal contamination.
